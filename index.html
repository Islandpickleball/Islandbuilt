San Juan Island Building

Island building is a different discipline. Ferries, limited trades, compressed seasons, and a permitting environment that rewards those who know how to navigate it.

We are selective. We are not the cheapest option on the island. We are available before your plans are drawn, while your vision is still forming — and we stay fully engaged until the last detail is right.

Client Fit

Are We Right for Each Other?

Island Built is the right fit if…
  • You’ve tried to find a builder here and know how hard it is to get one who communicates and delivers.
  • You’re ready for a meaningful project — custom home, significant remodel, or well-considered addition — and want someone in your corner from the very beginning.
  • You value transparent budgeting, honest schedules, and a contractor who tells you the truth.
  • You’ve seen the waitlists at the larger island builders and need someone at that quality level who is personally invested.
We may not be the right fit if…
  • You’re looking for the lowest bid on a small repair or handyman list.
  • You need a contractor who can begin an unplanned project next week.
  • Your primary goal is the lowest possible price, not the best possible outcome.

We respect your time enough to be honest about that upfront.
